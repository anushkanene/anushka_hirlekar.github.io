About Me

Hi! I'm Anushka.

A UX designer with a foundation in architecture and a passion for designing intuitive, human-centered digital experiences.

I began my creative journey as an architect in Mumbai, where I learned to balance structure, function, and aesthetics in physical spaces. It was through that work, watching how people moved through and experienced built environments, that I became fascinated by the digital equivalent: how interfaces shape behavior, how systems support people, and how thoughtful design can make technology feel natural.

After moving to the U.S., I enrolled in UC Berkeley's Professional Program in UX Design, where I've been building expertise in interaction design, visual design, and front-end development. My approach blends the analytical with the empathetic: I enjoy asking the right questions, uncovering real user needs, and translating those insights into designs that are purposeful and elegant.

I also enjoy exploring how tools like AI can enhance creative workflows, acting not as shortcuts, but as collaborators.

On a personal note, I'm happily married and a proud new mama — my baby boy arrived at the very start of 2026, and life has never felt more full. Becoming a parent has only deepened my belief in designing with empathy, for the moments that truly matter.
